The data in  ADL_proc_AG and FALL_proc_AG files are organized as follows:
+─────+─────+─────+─────+─────+─────+──────────+───────────+
| ax  , ay  , az  , gx  , gy  , gz  , SVM Acc  , SVM Gyro  |
+─────+─────+─────+─────+─────+─────+──────────+───────────+
where (ax,ay,az) are the tri-acceleration components of the acceleration sensor ADXL345.
and (gx,gy,gz) are the components of the gyro sensor ITG3200.
